About

Starting my career as an accountant, working with large spreadsheets and tinkering with complicated formulas was what interested me most. It was easy for me to get turned on to coding and tools that can easily gobble up datasets that would leave an excel spreadsheet spinning into a blue circle of death. Rather than click around in a spreadsheet doing a task that might take 30 minutes a week - can I write some code that does this automatically? Then I'll get 25 hours back over the course of a year. It doesn't take much for a bunch of columns and rows to get my curiosity going. What's the story behind this? How can I most effectively tease it out? Bool is my favorite data type. I love the elegant on/off simplicity. Along with the tilde (~) operator in python, I'm in heaven.
